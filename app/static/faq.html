Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this site?
This news application website was constructed as part of PeopleStreme Deployment Team's Internal Projects to enhance developers' experience with AngularJS.
Are the news articles dummy articles?
The news articles are 100% authentic and have been sourced from established news agencies worldwide with the help of NewsAPI Service. However, there is no guarantee of the accuracy of facts portrayed in the said articles.
Why can't I read the articles in detail, instead of just viewing article highlights and stubs?
The service that this app relies on to fetch the headline articles only offers this feature to paid subscription. This app was written with the developer (limited) mode of the API. Hence, full article contents are not available.
Why are some images missing?
NewsStreme is not responsible for checking the integrity of the image source links.
