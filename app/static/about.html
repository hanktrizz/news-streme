About & Terms of Service

This site was developed as an exercise in AngularJS for the V6 team by Thi Han Kyaw.

Currently, it offers news articles on a as-is basis with total reliance on the third party NewsAPI. There are currently no provisions and contingency in the event of failure on NewsAPI"s end. There are, however, plans to improve on the robustness of the app if time permits. News from sources are not verified prior to usage and manipulation in this app.

The app was developed to meet the minimum requirements and does not currently have any other features outside of Featuring articles, organising and grouping news type (World/Australian/Local/All).
